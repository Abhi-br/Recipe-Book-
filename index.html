Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water

Recipe

  1. Combine milk powder, flour, ghee, and milk to form dough.
  2. Shape dough into small balls, and fry balls in oil until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with sugar, water, and cardamom and soak fried balls in the syrup.
  4. Serve warm or at room temperature. Enjoy!

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk powder
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • ½ cup ghee
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee, add milk powder.
  2. Stir in condensed milk and cook until thick.
  3. Mix nuts and spread in a greased tray.
  4. Cool, cut into squares and it is ready for serve!

Peda

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk powder
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee, add milk powder.
  2. Stir in condensed milk and cook until thick.
  3. Mix in cardamom and nuts.
  4. Shape into small discs. Cool and serve!

Khir

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 100g sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)

Recipe

  1. Simmer milk on low heat, stirring occasionally, until reduced by half.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder; cook for 5 more minutes.
  3. Chill and serve, garnished with more nuts. Enjoy!

Modak

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup jaggery

Recipe

  1. Prepare dough with rice flour and water.
  2. Cook coconut and jaggery until melted.
  3. Shape dough into cups, fill with coconut mixture.
  4. Steam for 10 minutes. Serve warm and enjoy!

Custard Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk (for chenna)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for syrup)
  • 2 tablespoons custard powder
  • 1 cup milk (for custard)

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, curdle to make chenna; drain and knead..
  2. Shape into balls; boil in sugar syrup.
  3. Prepare custard with custard powder and milk.
  4. Soak rasgullas in custard. Chill and serve!
